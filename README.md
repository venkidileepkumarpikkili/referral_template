# referral_template
Great question! The timing of a referral request depends on the company’s hiring process, but here’s how to approach it strategically:

### **When to Ask for a Referral:**
1. **Before the Job is Posted (Ideal)**
   - If you know a company is hiring (e.g., through your network or industry news), ask for a referral *before* the role is officially posted.
   - Example: *"I heard [Company] is expanding the [Team Name] team—would you be open to referring me if a role opens up?"*

2. **After the Job is Posted (Common)**
   - If the role is already live, ask for a referral *as soon as possible* (within 1-2 days of posting).
   - Example: *"I saw the [Job Title] role at [Company]—would you be willing to refer me? I’d really appreciate it!"*

3. **During the Hiring Process (Less Common but Possible)**
   - If you’ve already applied but want to boost your chances, a referral can still help.
   - Example: *"I applied for the [Job Title] role—would you be open to referring me? I’d love any support you can offer."*

### **Key Tips:**
- **Ask early**—referrals are most impactful before the hiring manager reviews applications.
- **Be polite and low-pressure**—give your connection an easy "out" (e.g., *"No worries if it’s not possible!"*).
- **Follow up**—if they agree, send them your resume and a thank-you note.

### **Referral Request Message**
**Subject:** Quick Favor – Referral for [Job Title] at [Company Name]

Hi [Connection’s Name],

I hope you're doing great! I noticed [Company Name] is hiring for a [Job Title] role, and I’d love to apply. Since you’re connected to [Company Name], I was wondering if you’d be open to referring me or sharing any insights about the hiring process.

I’d really appreciate any guidance you can offer—I’m confident my experience in [relevant skill/achievement] would make me a strong fit. Let me know if you’d like to chat!

Thanks so much for your time, and no pressure at all!

Best,
[Your Full Name]
[Your LinkedIn Profile] (optional)

---

### **Tips for Success:**
- **Personalize both messages** (mention something specific about the job or your connection).
- **Keep it concise** (recruiters and connections are busy).
- **Send follow-ups 1-2 weeks after applying** (not too soon, not too late).
